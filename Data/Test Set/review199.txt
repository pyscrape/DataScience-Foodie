If you are a foodie who loves pasta, you need to come to this place! 
Authentic, homemade pasta. Everyday is a different dish they feature. It's a small restaurant so get their early. 
Although even on busy days, the service is fast and friendly. 
Let's talk about the food. It was beyond delicious. Everything about it was so tasty. I loved their kale salad! So good! 
I swear, after you read this, Go now!
